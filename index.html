Government Schemes

Schemes Made Simple

9 major government programs explained clearly. Find out what you're eligible for and how to apply — in minutes.

DIRECT BENEFIT

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers, in three installments of ₹2,000.

Eligibility: Farmers with up to 2 hectares of land
Benefit: ₹6,000/year directly to bank account
Learn & Apply
DIGITAL

Digital Agriculture Mission

India's flagship program to create a digital ecosystem for agriculture using AI, IoT, and satellite data.

Eligibility: All registered farmers
Benefit: Free digital tools & training
Learn More
DATA STACK

AgriStack

A unified farmer database creating digital identities for all Indian farmers to seamlessly access services.

Eligibility: All land-owning farmers
Benefit: Unified digital farmer ID
Register Now
MARKETPLACE

eNAM (National Market)

Electronic trading portal connecting farmers directly to buyers nationwide, eliminating middlemen and maximizing profits.

Eligibility: Farmers in 1000+ mandis
Benefit: Better market prices
Start Trading
INSURANCE

PMFBY – Crop Insurance

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana protects farmers against crop loss due to drought, flood, pest attack, or natural calamities.

Eligibility: All crop-growing farmers
Benefit: Full crop loss compensation
Get Insured
SOIL HEALTH

Soil Health Card

Free soil testing with personalized fertilizer recommendations to optimize crop yields and reduce unnecessary chemical use.

Eligibility: All farming households
Benefit: Free soil testing + card
Get Tested
CREDIT

Kisan Credit Card

Flexible credit facility for farmers to meet agricultural expenses — seeds, fertilizers, equipment — at subsidized interest rates.

Eligibility: Farmers, tenant farmers, SHGs
Benefit: Credit up to ₹3 lakh at 4%
Apply for KCC
MOBILE

mKisan Portal

SMS-based advisory service delivering personalized farming tips, market prices, and weather alerts directly to farmer phones.

Eligibility: Any farmer with a mobile
Benefit: Free SMS advisories
Subscribe
SEEDS

SATHI – Seed Portal

Transparent seed supply chain management ensuring farmers get authentic, quality-tested seeds at fair prices.

Eligibility: All registered farmers
Benefit: Subsidized quality seeds
Order Seeds
